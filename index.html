Advanced Seal Shape

Wide Bridge Seal & UV Protection

Most kids' goggles leak because their bridge and suction surface areas are too narrow. Swim Nori utilizes a premium wide-bridge layout previously reserved for expensive adult racing goggles. This distributes seal suction safely across more facial surface area, dramatically reducing leaking and pressure rings.

  • UV
    Crystal Clear UVA & UVB Protection

    Anti-Fog Coated Lenses

    Keeps water droplets flat on the lens so children don't feel tempted to take goggles off to wipe them.
